Current research interests.

My current research focuses on agentic AI in data science, on how AI can improve the quality and efficiency of investment decisions, and on how AI can support better content creation with lower labor cost.

Working Paper

From Human Workflow to Agentic Workflow: Transitional Scaffolding with the R Package agentr

This paper introduces agentr, an R-based framework for turning tacit expert workflow into explicit design artifacts that can be reviewed and revised.

It makes three main contributions: a framing of agent building as transitional scaffolding, a two-axis design model separating subsystem choice from workflow structure, and an implementation with before-and-after refinement demonstrations.
